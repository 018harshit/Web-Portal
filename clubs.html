ABESEC is committed to helping students get most out of their experience at the college.

These clubs provide students an apt platform to have fun and showcase talent. College offers endless opportunities beyond the academic programme and classroom setup for the participation and leadership of students. It helps in the transformation and holistic development of the students. The clubs are particularly aimed at helping students to get involved and find their own area in campus.

Creative U Club

The Creative U Club at ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad aims to build a community that lays the foundation to nurture artistic skills & a gateway to connect with the larger art community. The vision of the club is to spread the joy of creation, bring artists on a common platform together and foster the idea of art thus, creating enriched opportunities and experiences..

Aagaz - Publication Club

To bring out the hidden and innovative talent in college through their work by our publication. Student’s thought, ideas, innovation publish in the AAGAZ magazine.Creativity is the single most desirable attribute of an engineer. The general impression of engineers is doing things by the book, rather than writing the rulesBeing an engineer is not limited to being innovative or creative but it is a necessity for the improvement of the society and that is the primary job of an engineer.

Cultural Club

Cultural Club is not only about dancing and singing ,it is about the varied cultures which an individual can display by his/ her passion in the form of dancing , singing and many other manifold fields . We also aim to get more recognition for Fashion show, rapping, beat boxing , quiz and various kinds of painting too. There’s a talent hidden in each one of us and we’re providing you the opportunity to follow your talent. Never stop yourself from following your passion.

Environ Club

Environ Club is a step towards Greener and Healthier environment in college. A platform where students use their knowledge to tackle environmental issues and learn about the environment from the environment.

NSS Club

Picturesque Club

PICTURESQUE always gives all students of ABESEC an opportunity irrespective of their photography equipment, affiliations or experience. It is the only platform that provides students to be a part of all events happening in the college

Spic Macay

SPIC MACAY introduced of the lec-dem (lecture-demonstration) format to present Indian classical art forms in a comprehensive manner in front of today’s youth, which has given them an understanding of the basics of the classical art forms, and done away with the misconception that classical arts are difficult to understand.

Minerva Literature Club

Since its inception, MINERVA-The Literary Society of ABES-EC has been trying to create an environment that fosters uninhibited expression of thoughts and ideas and has successfully achieved this endeavour. The society is not only an integral part of the college’s semblance but has also become the choice of many students due to its crisp yet comfortable working environment that brings out the best in each of its members.

SYC & Yoga Club
